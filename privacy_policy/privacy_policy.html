      gross domestic prodactivity

      money

      you may consume money when running a domestic command.

      foods

      you will consume foods when playing battle.

      dev

      increaing anything productivity for the nation randomly.

      marchandise

      sell or buy anything(money, foods, irons, horses, gunpowders) randomly.

      hire

      hireing a anything unit randomly.

      this is popup displaying the information when tapped any emblem.

      the unit's attack power is low performance when the value is low, and vice versa.

      MP

      militaly power(number of soldiers) the unit's attack is easy to miss on any enemy when the value is low, and vice versa.
